What is egg tart (Cantonese called Daat Tart)?

One of the world-famous pastries in Hong Kong! Hong Kong egg tart is unlike European custard tarts. It is a variation of the British custard tart. Hong Kong-style egg tarts consist of an eggy custard centre with a pastry crust. There are many bakeries and dim sum restaurants that prepare their tarts using a flaky puff pastry crust with multiple layers. However, some bakers pay homage to the tart's Western history by using a buttery shortbread crust.
