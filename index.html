Pineapple Bun is the most iconic food in Hong Kong

What is Pineapple Bun?

The history of Pineapple bun are a Hong Kong backery classtic - a fluffy, milk bread-based bun topped with a crackly, crunchy cookie toping. The name pineapple bun doesn't come from any pineapple additions to the bun but its shape

Hightlights you might missed!

  • Fresh from the oven with a crispy crown that does not easily fall off
  • Often served warm with a thick slice of cold butter in the middle. Additional variations include versions with custard and red bean.
  • light, soft, and airy, with an extra crispy, buttery, and sugary crust that cracks perfectly into a pineapple pattern.
